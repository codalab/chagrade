Welcome to Chagrade

Here you can enroll in classes as a student or instruct and manage them as an instructor.
Sign Up

Upload:

Upload your data and baseline methods. The platform lets you choose among pre-loaded datasets or lets you upload your own in a simple text-based format. The platform also lets you upload a starting kit to help the participants solve there tasks. A Python example is provided.

Design:

Prepare your very own challenge protocol and rules. The platform helps you create tasks by automatically splitting your data and letting you choose among pre-defined metrics. An editor helps you add documentation pages.

Ship:

The platform compiles your tasks, sample code, and documentation into a self-contained zip archive, ready to upload to a competition platform. (in this version, Codalab.org is supported).
